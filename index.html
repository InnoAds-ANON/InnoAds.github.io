InnoAds: A Foundation Dataset and Benchmark for Advertising Visual Effects in Video Generation

Anonymous Author

ABSTRACT

Advertising visual effects (Ad-VFX) are the significant visual elements of ad- vertising videos that combine dynamic product presentation with accompanying descriptive text. However, research on Ad-VFX has been hindered by the lack of dedicated datasets and standardized evaluation protocols, as it is still an emerging domain. To address this issue, we introduce InnoAds-32K, a foundation dataset of over 32,000 curated image–text–video triples tailored for advertising scenarios. Furthermore, we propose InnoAds-Bench, a comprehensive benchmark that spans six evaluation dimensions: visual quality and text relevance as general metrics, and motion, product consistency, text stability, and creative rationality as advertising- specific metrics. Based on this suite, we systematically evaluate state-of-the-art video generation models, revealing substantial limitations in their ability to satisfy advertising requirements. In summary, InnoAds-32K and InnoAds-Bench provide the first standardized foundation for Ad-VFX video generation, paving the way for future research in advertising scenarios.

INNOADS-32K CATEGORIES

InnoAds-32K is a collection of over 32,000 curated image–text–video triples focused on advertising scenarios. It spans 44 fine-grained categories organized into six major groups: Electronics & Mobility, Lifestyle & Leisure, Food & Beverages, Beauty & Health, Fashion & Luxury, and Home & Appliances.

INNOADS-32K PIPELINE

To construct InnoAds-32K, we develop a multi-stage pipeline to ensure the quality and reliability of advertising-specific video content, consisting of SKU collection and filtering, image collection and filtering, prompt construction, image-to-video generation, video filtering, and metadata supplementation.

INNOADS-BENCH STATISTICS

InnoAds-Bench comprises 176 cases across six major domains. The distribution includes Home & Appliances, Food & Beverages, Beauty & Health, Fashion & Luxury, Electronics & Mobility, and Lifestyle & Leisure.

Figure 4: Example cases from different categories in InnoAds-Bench.

METRICS FOR AD-VFX

We design evaluation metrics for AD-VFX in six dimensions: two general ones evaluated with AesScore and GmeScore, and four advertising-specific ones measured by MotionXScore, ProductScore, TSScore, and CRScore. These metrics provide a comprehensive basis for assessing advertising video generation.
